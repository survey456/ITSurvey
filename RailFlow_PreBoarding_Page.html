About Us

Who We Are

A fast-growing freight-tech company shaping the future of sustainable logistics — with over 170 team members, 500+ customers, and a clear mission.

🚂

Our Company

We are a fast-growing freight-tech company with over 170 team members and more than 500 customers. In 2025, Rail-Flow and Simply Deliver joined forces, combining expertise, technology, and international spirit to create even more innovative solutions.

🌍

Our Mission

We simplify access to rail freight and intermodal transport through a neutral digital platform that connects all stakeholders, boosts efficiency, and drives the shift from road to rail — reducing CO₂ emissions and supporting climate goals.

Our Products

What We Offer

Two powerful product pillars that serve the entire rail logistics ecosystem.

Marketplace

Marketplace Solutions

Our marketplace makes it easy for companies to connect with the right logistics partners — bringing together those who need transport capacity with those who provide it, ensuring efficient collaboration and optimal resource use.

SaaS

SaaS Solutions

Our Software as a Service products — including our Transport Management System (TMS) — support the entire logistics process from inquiry and order management to billing, plus collaboration and CRM tools for all stakeholders.
